Old — global max angle For each point triplet, compute the angle between direction vectors. Take the maximum across all triplets.

Problem: one accidental jag anywhere → score spikes to 1.
New — 5-segment avg of max angle Split the point array into 5 equal chunks. Compute max angular sharpness within each chunk. Final score = RMS of the 5 maxima (quadratic mean — high values pull harder).

One bad spike only poisons 1/5 of the score.
